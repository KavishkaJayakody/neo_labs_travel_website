Horton Plains is a national park located in the central highlands of Sri Lanka. It is situated in the Nuwara Eliya District and is part of the Central Highlands UNESCO World Heritage site. Here's some information about Horton Plains: Location: Horton Plains National Park is located about 32 kilometers (20 miles) south of Nuwara Eliya, a popular hill station in Sri Lanka. It is situated at an elevation of about 2,100 to 2,300 meters (6,900 to 7,500 feet) above sea level.
